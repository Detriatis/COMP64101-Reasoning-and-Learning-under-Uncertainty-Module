Front: What are Probabilistic Graphical Models (PGMs)?
Back: Graph structures used to represent conditional dependencies and independencies in a set of random variables.
Tags: PGMs, Intro
Front: In a PGM, what do nodes represent?
Back: Random variables.
Tags: PGMs, Intro
Front: In a PGM, what do edges represent?
Back: Conditional dependence.
Tags: PGMs, Intro
Front: In a PGM, what does the lack of an edge represent?
Back: Conditional independence (CI).
Tags: PGMs, Intro
Front: What are the main types of graphical models?
Back: Directed, Undirected, and combinations of both.
